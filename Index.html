Fencing is a sport that requires intense concentration and fast instincts. You also need to be able to make smart decisions in a small amount of time, which is why fencing is such a fun sport to pursue!

Fencing equipment

Fencing is a sport that requires many different epuipment. You need two layers of special fencing wear, a fencing mask, a wire, long socks, fencing shoes, special gloves, a plastic guard you wear in your clothes to keep you safe, and a sword.

Foil fencing Eppe fencing Sabre fencing

There are 3 different kinds of fencing; Foil, epee, and sabre. Personally, I compete in the foil competitions.

Fencing rules

Fencing has many rules that athletes need to stick to. If you foul, you might give your opponent a free point if you continue to make mistakes!
